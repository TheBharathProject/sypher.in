Merge Up

This page is the official homepage of the Merge Up mobile game by Shubham.

What this app does

Merge Up is a puzzle game where players merge numbered blocks to reach higher scores and unlock levels.

Why we collect data

We collect only game username and score. This is used for leaderboards and gameplay features. We do not collect personal identifiers like name or phone number.
